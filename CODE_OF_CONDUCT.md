# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being respectful and empathetic
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code.

Report issues to the maintainers via private communication.
